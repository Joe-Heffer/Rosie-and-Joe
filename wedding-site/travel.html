Getting Here

Travel & Directions

Eyam Hall sits in the historic village of Eyam in the Peak District.

By Car

Eyam Hall, Main Road,
Eyam, Hope Valley,
Derbyshire, S32 5QW

Eyam is easily reached from the M1 (junction 29) and A623. There is free parking in the village car park, a short walk from the hall. Please allow extra time for the scenic country roads.

By Public Transport

Nearest rail station: Grindleford (approx. 4 miles)
Bus services run from Sheffield and Bakewell to Eyam.

The nearest railway stations are Grindleford and Hathersage, both on the Sheffield–Manchester line. Local buses connect the surrounding towns to Eyam village. Taxis are limited, so we recommend booking in advance.
