Evening Celebration

We can't wait to dance the night away with you.

Join us from 7:00 pm for a relaxed evening of drinks, food and dancing at Eyam Hall. There's a bar serving drinks throughout the evening, food to keep you going, and live music to get everyone up on the floor. Come ready to celebrate with us into the night.
