Our wedding meal menu is fully vegetarian and plant-based. Please let us know on your RSVP if you have any other dietary requirements.

Good to Know

A Few Notes from the Venue

A handful of house rules from Eyam Hall, so nothing comes as a surprise on the day:

  • The courtyard — Eyam Hall is open to the public during the day, so you may spot other visitors around the courtyard during drinks and photographs. It's ours exclusively from 5 pm, once the Hall closes for the day.
  • Confetti & sparklers — natural petal confetti (fresh or dried) is welcome outside, but please leave plastic confetti at home — the venue charges a £100 fee for it. Sparklers are also welcome outside; Chinese lanterns and drones aren't permitted.
  • Children — little ones are very welcome. The grounds include a water feature, so we'd ask parents to keep an eye on them.
  • Four-legged friends — well-behaved dogs are welcome by prior arrangement. Please let us know in advance if you'd like to bring one.
  • Smoking — designated outdoor areas only, including e-cigarettes. All indoor spaces are non-smoking.
  • Bar — drinks are on us from the end of the ceremony through to the end of the wedding meal. After that, the bar switches to pay-as-you-go by cash or card for the evening celebration.
  • End of the night — music finishes at 11.30 pm and the bar closes at midnight, by which time everyone needs to have headed off. There's no Uber out in the Peaks, so please pre-book your taxi — see our Getting Here page.
