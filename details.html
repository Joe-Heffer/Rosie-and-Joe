The Day

Order of the Day

Everything you need to know about our celebration at Eyam Hall.

Ceremony

1:00 pm

Eyam Hall

Eyam Hall, Main Road,
Eyam, Hope Valley,
Derbyshire, S32 5QW

Join us as we exchange our vows in the beautiful surroundings of Eyam Hall. Please aim to arrive by 12:30 pm so we can begin promptly. Guests will be seated in the main hall.

Reception

3:00 pm until late

The Courtyard & Barn

Eyam Hall Courtyard,
Eyam, Hope Valley,
Derbyshire, S32 5QW

Following the ceremony, we'll celebrate with drinks in the courtyard, a wedding breakfast, speeches and dancing into the evening. Expect good food, good music and plenty of dancing.

Dress Code

Formal

What to Wear

We'd love for you to dress up with us — think formal/black-tie optional. As parts of the day take place outdoors, we recommend bringing a layer for the spring weather and shoes suitable for grassy ground.
